\section*{Declaration of generative AI and AI-assisted technologies in the manuscript preparation process}

The intellectual contribution of this work---including the original
concept, the design of the EMMET-DP algorithm, the physical
motivation, the experimental methodology, the choice of baselines,
topologies, and metrics, the interpretation of results, and all
editorial decisions---is the author's. The author does not write code,
so Claude (Anthropic) was used as a programming assistant to implement
the algorithm and experiments according to the author's specifications,
and as a writing assistant for editorial refinement of selected
passages. The author additionally used Codex (OpenAI) and Kimi
(Moonshot AI) as independent reviewers for iterative auditing of the
manuscript and code. Information queries about prior art, datasets,
and topology libraries (e.g.\ Internet Topology Zoo, GÉANT, Abilene)
were resolved through these assistants and verified by the author from
primary sources. The author reviewed, validated, and tested all
generated content, and takes full responsibility for the published
work.
